Privacy Policy for YEYO HUB

Last updated: February 26, 2026

Camera Permission

YEYO HUB requests access to your device's camera for the following purposes:

  • Proof of Delivery: Drivers can capture photos as proof that deliveries have been completed.
  • Expense Receipts: Photograph receipts for fuel, tolls, and other trip expenses.
  • Vehicle Condition: Document vehicle condition before and after trips.
  • Incident Reporting: Capture images of accidents or incidents for insurance purposes.

Photos are uploaded to secure company servers and are only accessible to authorized fleet managers and dispatchers. Images are retained for audit and record-keeping purposes as required by law.

Location Data

YEYO HUB collects precise GPS location data in the foreground and background to enable real-time vehicle tracking, route optimization, and trip verification. Location data is shared with fleet managers and dispatchers for operational purposes.

Data Collection

We collect:

  • Name, email, and phone number (for account management)
  • GPS location (for tracking)
  • Photos (for proof of delivery and expense reporting)
  • Device information (for troubleshooting)

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data is shared only with:

  • Fleet managers and dispatchers (for operational purposes)
  • Service providers who assist in app functionality
  • Legal authorities when required by law
